East African Music Festival, Kigali 2022

This Festival is aiming at providing a unique and once-in-a-lifetime musical experience in East Africa. This festival wil also make it possible for those who will attend the festival to experience a mixture of a stand-up comedy with live music in the backgroud.

29 October 2022 (sat)

Intare Conference Arena (Kabuga/Kigali)

Program

  • Live Music
  • listen to great Live Music both by prominent musicians as well as rising stars from the region
  • Comedy
  • get laughter and humor from the best comedians in East Africa.
  • Drinks
  • there will be a drinking session for free of charge
  • Diner
  • Have an amazing opportunity to share diner, exchange ideas and connect with the free spirited people like you.
  • After Party
  • Enjoy the night with alot of dancing, bonfire in an open nightclub like establishment
